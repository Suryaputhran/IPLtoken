1. Executive Summary

Welcome to 12M Token, the definitive Web3 ecosystem tailored for the hundreds of millions of Indian Premier League (IPL) fans. 12M introduces a revolutionary "Support-to-Earn" mechanism, transitioning passive cricket viewership into an active, rewarding experience.

By minting dynamic digital Player Cards representing their favorite IPL superstars, 12M holders are entitled to instant, automated cryptocurrency dividends generated directly by the underlying Treasury every time their chosen player hits a 6, takes a wicket, or their franchise wins a match.

2. The Problem & Solution

The Problem

Sports betting and daily fantasy leagues are highly popular but inherently risky. The vast majority of participants lose capital. To engage deeply with the game, fans are forced to gamble their hard-earned money.

The 12M Solution

12M Token introduces the Zero-Risk Fan Dividend. Fans purchase ecosystem tokens ($12M) and use them to mint Player Cards. Unlike betting, your player failing does not result in the loss of your card or capital; you simply retain the card for the next match.

Zero Capital Loss: You never lose your Player Card. If your player scores 0, your digital asset remains yours. If they score a century, you receive massive crypto payouts!

3. Core Mechanics (Support-to-Earn)

Minting Player Cards

During the TGE (Token Generation Event), fans can connect their Web3 wallets to the 12M dApp and spend $12M tokens to mint Player Cards spanning all 10 IPL franchises.

Yield Generation Triggers

The core Smart Contract is hooked into a reliable decentralised Oracle that feeds live match data during the IPL season. Yields are distributed based on the following metrics, direct to the holder's wallet:

  • Batting: Sixes Hit, Half-Centuries (50), Centuries (100)
  • Bowling: Wickets Taken, 3-Wicket Hauls, 5-Wicket Hauls
  • Match Outcome: Base Team Win

Rivalry Clashes & The Championship Yield

Fans can stake their cards against a friend's opposing card during live match hours. The holder of the winning franchise's player receives a 2x Rivalry Bonus for that match.

At the tournament's conclusion, an enormous portion of the Treasury—the Championship Yield—is distributed evenly among all users holding cards associated with the IPL Championship-winning franchise.
